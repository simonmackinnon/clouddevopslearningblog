## Comment Policy

Welcome to The Cloud DevOps Learning Blog, a blog about my journey learning things related to AWS and DevOps. Any comments that I consider trolling, spam or abuse will be deleted. 

Be polite – don’t be rude to anyone and respect others. 

Don’t troll. I appreciate and welcome criticism towards my content here, but comments that add no value to the discussion or that are merely insulting would be deleted without exception.

Spam? Goodbye. 

Please flag or 'mark as spam' any violating comments - I'll check them out to make sure they're kosher! 

Cheers!
